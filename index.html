Eg. QGIS Classification - field Calculation: (N:steps, fields from 0 to 100% values)

  • (0-100) stright axes | concat((round( "field_1" /(100/(N-1)))), '_', (round( "field_2" /(100/(N-1)))))
  • (100-0) reverse axes | concat((round((N-1) - "field_1" /(100/(N-1)))), '_', (round((N-1) -"field_2" /(100/(N-1)))))
  • mixed order axes | concat((round("field_1" /(100/(N-1)))), '_', (round((N-1) -"field_2" /(100/(N-1)))))
